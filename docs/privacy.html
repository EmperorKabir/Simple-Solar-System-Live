Privacy Policy

App: Simple Live Solar System · Effective: 4 May 2026

Summary

The Simple Live Solar System app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no internet access.

What data the app accesses

  • System time and time zone — read locally to compute current planet and moon positions. Never transmitted off-device.
  • Time-zone preference — when you change time zone in the app, your selection is saved in the WebView's local storage on your device. Never transmitted off-device.

What the app does NOT do

  • The app requests no Android permissions.
  • The app makes no network requests of any kind. There are no internet permissions in the manifest.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs that send data off-device.
  • No personal information, identifiers, location, contacts, or device data are collected.
  • No accounts, no sign-in.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone, no special handling is required for users under 13.

Open source

This app is open source. The full code is published at github.com/EmperorKabir/Simple-Solar-System-Live. You are welcome to inspect the network-isolation claims directly.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the effective date above will change and the new version will appear at the same URL.
